Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our web design services

How long does it take to build a website?
Our typical timeline is 2-4 weeks for Starter packages, 3-5 weeks for Business packages, and 4-6 weeks for Premium packages, depending on project complexity and client feedback.
Do you provide website hosting?
Yes, all our packages include 1 year of premium hosting with 99.9% uptime guarantee, SSL certificate, and daily backups. Renewal is AED 600/year after the first year.
Can I update the website myself?
Absolutely! Our Business and Premium packages include CMS integration (WordPress) so you can easily update content without technical knowledge. We also provide training.
Do you offer custom packages?
Yes, we understand that every business is unique. Contact us for a custom quote tailored to your specific requirements, timeline, and budget constraints.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept bank transfers, credit cards, and installment plans for larger projects. A 50% deposit is required to start any project, with the balance due upon completion.
Do you provide ongoing maintenance?
Yes, we offer monthly maintenance plans starting from AED 300/month for updates, security monitoring, performance optimization, and technical support.
